A Occhio Nudo
Project info
Editorial Design
Team
S. Buffa, A. Cupisti,
E. Galliani, P. Pascuzzi, G. Polimeno
Year
2021
Transmedia translation of Blindness, a novel by José Saramago. The project was created during the Sociology of Cultural and Communication Processes course at the Politecnico di Milano, with the intention of rewriting the story by involving, through the use of various graphic devices, the only sense missing from the narrative: sight. The reader, not only follows on several levels what blindness narrates, but is also guided by it through pages that simulate the asphyxiation and suffocating sensations of the events that punctuate the story. To do this, we experimented with graphics and typography, often manipulating them to create the desired visual effects. A Occhio Nudo was exhibited at ELO 2022, an international conference on electronic literature. The book was also selected by the Neologia project and exhibited during the 8th Graphic Days®️ in Turin .

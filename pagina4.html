Endless Coffee
Project info
Modelling
3D animation
Team
E. Galliani, C. Tosi, G. Polimeno
Year
2022
The Endless Coffee is a 3D animation created during the Computer Animation course, featuring a micro-world within a coffee machine, where a coffee bean embarks on a journey through slides, roller coasters, and neon-lit passages. Every element in this animation was meticulously modeled, bringing the coffee machine’s internal universe to life. The narrative unfolds in a loop: the operator of the mechanisms drinks the same coffee at the beginning that eventually gushes into the cup. This final scene seamlessly connects to the initial scene, creating a paradox where more coffee is needed to produce coffee.

Endless Coffee
Project info
3D modelling
3D animation
Collaborators
E. Galliani, C. Tosi
Year
2022
The Endless Coffee is a 3D animation of a representation of a micro-world inside a coffee machine, where a coffee bean finds itself in world of slides, roller coasters, and neon lights. The narrative is structured as a loop: the controller of the mechanisms drinks the same coffee at the beginning that eventually gushes into the cup. The final scene thus corresponds to the initial scene generating a paradox, according to which more coffee is needed to produce coffee.

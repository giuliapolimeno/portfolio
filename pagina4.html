Life Bites
Project info
Video production
Year
2023
Some of my personal videos, that encapsulate the essence of concepts like "the sweetness of doing nothing", or "get human". Through these visual narratives, I aim to enrich the idea of embracing the beauty found in seemingly ordinary moments, promoting a connection with the simple joys of life that often go unnoticed. Each video is a record of the art of storytelling through the lens of everyday experiences, inviting viewers to appreciate the poetry woven into the fabric of our daily lives.

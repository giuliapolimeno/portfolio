BCTR
Project info
Speculative Design
Team

Year
2023
This speculative project, created for my final bachelor’s laboratory and presented at the Triennale di Milano exhibition "Anthropogenic Narratives" , explores the relationship between humans and nature in the context of climate change, with a unique twist: it introduces non-human perspectives through the lens of the fictional BCTR corporation. BCTR is a secretive scientific organization that claims to reveal the true power of bacterial species, asserting their superiority over other life forms. The storytelling was enriched by the creation of maps of the BCTR headquarters and kits for its employees, further immersing users in this speculative universe.
At the forefront of their discoveries is Maya, an interactive electronic device designed to explore and communicate BCTR’s findings. Maya's primary function is to uncover the species best suited for the continuation of life on Earth, exposing a disturbing new reality. Users can interact with this device, which was prototyped using 3D printing and NFC technology. This project, which also serves as the subject of my bachelor’s thesis, delves into themes of environmentalism, scientific ethics, and the complex dynamics between humans and the natural world. Through Maya and the narrative surrounding BCTR, my team and I explored provocative questions about the future of life on Earth and our role within it.

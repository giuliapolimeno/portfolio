TEDx is able to compose reflections of fields seemingly disconnected from one another into something that is more than the isolated parts, allowing us to understand reality in its totality. Platonic solids, the most basic units of the cosmos for excellence, represent the natural possibility of change while not losing their ability to be independent and part of a whole. Tedx Courmayeur 2023, takes place in special natural setting, in an evocative high atmosphere surrounded by peaks, as if to emphasize that the edge between nature and geometry is almost imperceptible. The team and I, have created a system of artifacts to promote the event and these principles, from instagram posts to the merchandising.
